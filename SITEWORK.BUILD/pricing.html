Questions
Straight Answers
What does the setup fee cover?
The setup fee covers building your website from scratch, configuring your hosting, optimizing your Google Business Profile, and completing initial SEO setup. It's a one-time cost paid before we start — your first monthly payment begins when your site goes live.
How long until my site is live?
Most sites go live within 7 days of receiving your setup fee and onboarding information. We move fast because your time matters.
Is there a long-term contract?
No. We recommend giving local SEO at least 90 days to show results — that's just how Google works — but there's no lock-in. You can cancel anytime with 30 days notice.
What types of businesses do you work with?
We work with all types of local service businesses, but we specialize in the trades — HVAC, plumbing, electrical, roofing, and general construction. That specialization is what makes the difference.
When will I start seeing results from SEO?
Month 1 we build the foundation. By month 3 you'll typically see movement in rankings. By month 6, meaningful results. SEO is a long game — but it compounds and pays off far longer than any ad spend.
Do I own my website?
Yes. Your website, your domain, your content. If you ever leave, we hand everything over. No hostage-taking.
